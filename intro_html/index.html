Snowy the Cat

Snowy Facts

Snowy is a white short haired cat, she has green eyes and long whiskers. She is a calm and cuddly cat who has a tiny pink nose.She knows how to do a highfive as a trick.

More Snowy Facts

Snowy loves to nap in warm sunny spots, and likes to cuddle, she also loves eating bread. She also loves to talk to you about your day. She will watch tv waith you or hang around as you do your school work. Snowy loves to play with strings.
