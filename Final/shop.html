Doll Care

Caring for Monster High Dolls' Hair To care for your Monster High doll's hair, follow these steps:Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle the hair, avoiding fine-tooth combs that can damage the fibers.2:Rinse the hair in lukewarm water to prevent damage to the synthetic fibers. 3:Clean with Dawn dish soap and apply conditioner for a smoother finish. 4:Avoid using heat tools, as they can permanently alter the structure of synthetic fibers. 5:For advanced styling, consider braid styles or clips to maintain the doll's hair shape after cleaning. Remember, gentle handling and proper care are essential to preserving the doll's appearance and quality.

1. Gentle handling As with any fragile collectible, handle your Monster High doll with care. Avoid pulling or tugging on their limbs, hair, or clothing as this can cause damage. When playing with or moving your doll, use gentle motions and ensure that you’re not putting too much pressure on their joints. 2. Keep it clean Dust and dirt can accumulate on your Monster High doll over time. To avoid any damage caused by these particles, use a soft cloth or makeup brush to gently brush away any dust on the surface – including the face, limbs, and clothing. For stubborn stains on their clothes, remove the garments and carefully hand-wash them in cold water with mild detergent. 3. Protect from sunlight Excessive exposure to direct sunlight can cause fading and discoloration of your dolls and their clothing. Make sure to store or display your Monster High collection away from windows or areas where direct sunlight is present.
